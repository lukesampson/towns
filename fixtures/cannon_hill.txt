'''Cannon Hill''' is an [[inner suburb]] of [[Brisbane]], [[Queensland]], [[Australia]] located in the [[City of Brisbane]]. It is located {{convert|7|km|mi|}} east of the [[Brisbane CBD]].

== History ==
Cannon Hill is thought to have be named by a surveyor who noted down that two tree stumps resembled a cannon. More likely Cannon Hill was named after the Weedon family home 'Cannon Hill House'. Built in 1867, the house, which had extensive grounds and a maze, burnt down in 1927.&lt;ref&gt; [http://www.ourbrisbane.com/living/suburbs/cannon_hill/history/ Brisbane Suburb: Cannon Hill - History of Cannon Hill - ourbrisbane.com&lt;!-- Bot generated title --&gt;]&lt;/ref&gt; This house was build opposite of the present day primary school. &lt;ref name=UNIofQLD&gt;[http://queenslandplaces.com.au/cannon-hill]&lt;/ref&gt;

The railway was opened in 1889 going though Cannon Hill to Cleveland. At the same time blocks of land near the station were offered for sale. 

== Education ==
=== Schools ===
Cannon Hill contains the [[Cannon Hill State School]] and [[Cannon Hill Anglican College]]

== Commercial ==
=== Shops ===
Cannon Hill is home to many shops and restaurants. Major shopping centres include the [[Cannon Hill Shopping Plaza]] and Urban Village Cannon Hill.

== Transport ==
Cannon Hill is well serviced by [[public transport]] and is located in zones 2 and 3 of the [[TransLink (South East Queensland) | TransLink]] integrated public transport system. Cannon Hill is also serviced by two major roads which allow for easy transportation around the city.
=== Train ===
[[Cannon Hill railway station, Brisbane|Cannon Hill railway station]] provides access to regular [[Citytrain]] services to the [[Brisbane CBD]] and [[Cleveland railway station|Cleveland]].

=== Bus ===
The [[Cannon Hill bus station]] is a major interchange for the area. Bus routes include to the [[Brisbane CBD]], [[Chermside, Queensland | Chermside]], DFO and [[Carindale, Queensland | Carindale]].

=== Road ===
State Route 20 (Creek Road) - Connects Cannon Hill to [[Mount Gravatt, Queensland | Mount Gravatt]] and [[Murarrie, Queensland | Murarrie]]&lt;br/&gt;
State Route 23 (Wynnum Road, Lytton Road, Shafston Road) - Connects Cannon Hill to [[Kangaroo Point, Queensland | Kangaroo Point]] and [[Tingalpa, Queensland | Tingalpa]]
